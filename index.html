About

Project Manager, Research Manager, and Director with 15+ years spanning private sector consulting, policy research, academia, and AI-driven transformation — delivering multi-country market intelligence, partnership strategy, and go-to-market execution for governments, MNCs, and SMEs across Asia, Africa, and the Middle East. Combines team leadership (managed 25-person analyst teams), doctoral-level research expertise (PhD, Waseda University), and operational skills in building systems, strategic partnerships, and AI workflows.

Managed 25-person analyst teams producing 100+ industry reports annually for institutional investors. Built operational infrastructure including cost-management systems, SOP libraries, and research networks across 20+ countries. Developed company-wide AI integration strategies using LLMs, agentic AI, and prompt engineering. Sourced strategic partnerships across India, KSA, and Mauritius enabling geographic expansion.

Eight years as Associate Faculty at SUSS and SIM-GE — teaching ~100 students annually across Bachelor's and Master's programmes affiliated with University of London and UCL, managing academic performance evaluation and curriculum delivery. PhD in Infrastructure Development and Financing from Waseda University, Japan. Policy research at ADBI (Low Carbon Green Growth in Asia), UN Global Compact (sustainable finance strategy), and ERIA (private financing for low-carbon energy transition). Contributor to ADBI working papers and book chapters, ERIA discussion papers, CAREC Institute studies, SCMP, East Asia Forum, and Global Policy.
